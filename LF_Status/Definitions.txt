Labor Force Status Definitions:

Overtime - Working 50 hours or more per week.
Full-Time - Working between 36 and 49 hours per week.
Part-Time - Working 35 hours or less per week.
Unemployed - On unemployment insurance or seeking work while not working.
Taking care of home - Not working for pay and taking care of the house/family.
School - Not working for pay because they are attending school.
Retired - Not working for pay due to retirement.
Other - Not working for pay for other reasons not included in this list.
Disabled - Not working for pay due to a disability.
